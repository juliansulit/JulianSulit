Welcome to City of Bogo

The Hidden Gem of Cebu

Bogo City is located in the Northern Part of Cebu Province, Around 100 kilometers from Cebu City
It serves as a major transportation and commercial hub in the north.

Bogo City celebrates The Pintos Festival is a celebration of life and abundance in Bogo City
It is a thanksgiving for the blessings of nature, believed to come through the help of St. Vincent Ferrer
The city's patron saint. "Pintos" means "Beloved Saint".

The Our Lady of the Miraculous Medal Shrine in Bogo City is a peaceful place of worship dedicated to the Virgin Mary
People come to pray, seek blessings, and participate in religious events,
making it an important Spiritual center for the community.
